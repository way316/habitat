Land Animal Helping Hands

Australia's bushfire killed millions of animals and destroyed land animals' habitats. We must protect and help the surviving animals. We need to be well prepared to be ready to help the affected animal whenever bushfires happen again in the future. The tool helps volunteers and organizations find new homes for animals whose habitats have been destroyed .

Rescue

Search for the location of affected animal, rescue the animal by helping transfer the injured animals to nearest shelter.

Feeding

Provide water and food for the animals suffer from bushfire or donate money to maintain their habitat.

Volunteer

Join in some Animal Protection Orgnizations to ensure sustainable development of animals.
